# 1 Asia's polution exodus: Firms struggle to woo top talent

## 1.1 new word

1. `exodus [ˈeksədəs]`: （大批人的）退出，离开
    * > a situation in which many people leave a place at the same time
    * > ex-（往外；出去）
    * > the exodus from the countryside to the towns.
1. `woo [wuː]`: 争取；努力说服；求爱
    * > The store woos customers by offering low prices.
1. `perk [pɜːrk]`: （因工作而享有）补贴，额外待遇
    * > She only eats at the canteen because free meals are one of the perks of her job.
1. `bid [bɪd]`: 努力，尝试
    * > an attempt to achieve or get something
1. `lure [lʊr]`: 劝诱；诱惑
    * > to persuade or trick sb. to go somewhere or to do sth. by promising them a reward
    * > He was lured into the job by the offer of a high salary.
1. `engulf [ɪnˈɡʌlf]`: 完全包围；吞没
    * > en-（进入）
    * > gulf（深渊）
    * > to surround and cover something or someone completely
    * > The building was engulfed in flames.
    * > The despair treatened to engulf him
1. `put off`: 使反感；使对...失去兴趣
    * > to make sb. dislike sb. /sth.
    * > Don't be put off by how it looks-it tastes delicious.
1. `expertise [ˌekspɜːrˈtiːz]`: 专门知识；专门技能
    * > financial expertise
    * > medical expertise
1. `pose [poʊz]`: 造成，引起
    * > pose a threat
    * > pose problems
1. `incentive [ɪnˈsentɪv]`: 刺激；动力；鼓励
    * > motivation
    * > something that encourages a person to do something
    * > tax incentives
1. `allowance [əˈlaʊəns]`: （定期或处于特殊原因而给予的）一笔钱；津贴，补助
    * > a monthly allowance
    * > travel allowance
1. `derive from`: 来源于...，源自...
    * > to come or develop from something
    * > The word is derived from Latin.
1. `relocation [riˈloʊˈkeɪʃən]`: 重新安置
    * > re-（再次，重复）
    * > location（位置）
    * > office relocations
1. `incorporate [ɪnˈkɔːrpəreɪt]`: 包含
    * > to include something as part of something larger
1. `appealing [əˈpiːlɪŋ]`: appealing
    * > 有吸引力；令人感兴趣的
    * > attractive or interesting
1. `CV [kəˌrɪkjələm ˈviːtaɪ]`: 简历，履历
    * > curriculum vitae
